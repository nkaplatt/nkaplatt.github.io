Welcome to Duet

 

A dancer’s performance is more than just movement.  Clever costuming whether it’s a beautifully crafted ballet tutu or a quirky character outfit can make the difference between a well-executed routine and a confident and accomplished performance. 

At Duet we understand how important a costume is to a production and we want ours to contribute to the overall spectacle of the scene being portrayed whether this is a solo or a group performance.

Our aim is to provide high quality, hand-made costumes for dancers who are looking for something special.  Each of our costumes is made from hand-picked fabrics with the greatest attention to detail in decoration and embellishment.  Our designs are only limited by our and your own imaginations!
