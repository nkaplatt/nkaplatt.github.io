How to Order

A designed to order costume is made to a dancers measurements and requirements. As such, we prefer an initial consultation with you to discuss exactly what youre looking for and to take your measurements accurately.

We recognise that this can be difficult if you are out of our area so please do give us a call so that we can discuss alternative arrangements with you.
